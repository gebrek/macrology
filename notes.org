* On Lisp
** "Lisp Programs /are/ Data (Homoiconic)"
** Embedded Language
   A language built in such a way that the mother language (i.e. Lisp)
   can "appear unchanged" in the new language.
** Common Lisp has seperate namespaces for functions and data
   #+BEGIN_SRC lisp
     (defun double (x)
        (* x 2))
     (defvar double
       3)

     (double double) ; => 6
   #+END_SRC
